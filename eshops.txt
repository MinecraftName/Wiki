&6There are many shops on the server.
&6They are spread out on the map.
&6Always try find the cheapest deals.
&6Getting money is hard so you should try find the cheapest.

&6Server shops always have signs that start with &5[BUY]
&6On the second line you can find the amount your buying.
&6On the third line you see the item.
&7(Most of the times the item is also displayed above it!)
&6And on the last line you can find the price.

&6There are also player shops where shops aren't the same.
