&6Economy is something pretty important on the server.
&6You could play the server without using the economy system 
&6but earning money can be pretty easy.
&6Every player has a certain amount of money.
&6You probably know from other servers that you just have virtual money.
&6On this server you don't have that and the gold is the money.
&6So if you go to a shop you need to bring gold to the shop.
&6You can easy store this gold in one of the Banks
