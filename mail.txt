&6===== &5FantasyWar Help &8[&dName &71/1&8] &6=====

&6You can sent mail to other players.
&6You can just write a book and mail it to someone.
&6Hold the book you want to send and type this command.
&5/mail send {player}
&6Then a poster will come take the book.
&6He will bring it to the other player and he can pick it up.
&6If the other player is offline he will receive it later.
&6Remember that you mail the book with content.
&6This means you will lose the book unless he mails it back.
