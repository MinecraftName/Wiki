&6Empire chat means only your empire members can read it.
&6When there are wars and things with empire you use this chat.
&6It's good to discuss tactics and prepare and manage good wars.
&6This chat is a toggle instead of using a command.
&6You can just toggle it on and you can keep sending msges in it.
&6To toggle Empire chat use &5/e &6And then you can chat in it.
&6Use the same command to toggle it off again.

&6Example: &8/e &7(toggle on)
&6Example: &8Come to 453 -4359 for a big war vikings!
&6Example: &8/e &7(Toggle off again)
